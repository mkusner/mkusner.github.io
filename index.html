Matt J. Kusner

I am a research fellow at the Alan Turing Institute in London.
My work is in variational autoencoders, fair prediction, document distances, differential privacy, dataset compression, budgeted learning, and Bayesian optimization.

Previously I was a member of Kilian Weinberger's machine learning research group. CV, Contact

I was recently awarded the Turner Dissertation Award for best Computer Science & Engineering doctoral dissertation.
