Matt Kusner

I am an Associate Professor at École Polytechnique de Montréal and a Core Academic Member at Mila - Quebec Artificial Intelligence Institute.

My current research interests are in machine learning evaluation, testing, attacks, and defenses.

I am also a member of the European Laboratory for Learning and Intelligent Systems. Previously I was an Associate Professor at at University College London. Before this I was an Associate Professor at the University of Oxford. I was in the first group of Research Fellows at The Alan Turing Institute, and I did my Ph.D. with Kilian Weinberger. I am married to the wonderful Sonia Rego, and am a guardian of the fantastic Karl & Nora.
